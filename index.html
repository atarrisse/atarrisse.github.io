As Operations Manager for Comida da Gente, I’ve managed and run several collaborative purchases from small local producers. I'd look for new producers as well as welcome and support new producers when joining the community. I' keep an eye on their sales performance and analyze it. Whenever need, we'd come up with new strategies for each producer individually on how to improve. All the data was inputed on Spreadsheets and I came to develop scripts to aid some tasks.

Smart Living Challenge

Product Designer

The SLC is a initiative from the Swedish Institute held in 3 cities worldwide in 2015. The theme for Rio was mobility and it began with an open call for ideas, from which the 6 participants would be selected. Throughout 12 weeks we had several meetings, some of them with Hyper Island (Swedish consulting company), and worked on designing a scalable and modular conceptual project that could be implemented in the Federal University main campus in Rio. We had input from a very broad and fresh research about mobility in the campus. During the 12 weeks we analyzed the data and found out which issues were more urgent. We came up with a unified system of campus related information that would contemplate all its particularities. By the end of the process we presented it at the Embassy of Sweden in Brasília to relevant authorities on the field. Also, the Swedish Institute presented it as a part of their main presentation at the Sustainable Innovation Forum in Paris (COP21).
